Two failure modes to avoid simultaneously:
Drift — no anchor to present reality, living entirely inside future projections.
Stagnation — so absorbed in maintenance that no forward movement occurs.

This curriculum is built to hold both in tension. The morning grounds you in body and facts. The afternoon moves the work. The evening closes the loop and reorients. The anchor points interrupt the expansion-stagnation loop before it compounds.
PHASE 01
MORNING
BIOLOGICAL FIRST, EVERYTHING ELSE SECOND
0–90 min after waking
Light + movement before the screen
Natural light exposure within 30 min of waking. Walk outside if possible — even 10 minutes. This sets the circadian anchor that regulates every downstream system. No phone until this is done.
10–20 min
Somatic check-in — no interpretation
Sit still. Scan from feet to skull. Name what's present in the body without explaining it: tight, heavy, buzzing, numb, open. This is data collection, not journaling. Two minutes of honest sensing is worth more than twenty of analysis.
2–5 min
One orienting question — written
Write one sentence in response to: "What is the single most important thing I move toward today?" Not a list. One sentence. It anchors teleological direction (Adlerian: where is today's action pointing?) and keeps the archive-building instinct from becoming endless lateral expansion. If you can't answer it, that itself is information.
3–5 min
Breathwork — regulatory, not ritual
4-7-8 or box breathing (4-4-4-4). Purpose: bring the nervous system online before cognitive load begins. This is not spiritual practice — it is physiological priming. Extended exhale activates parasympathetic brake. You know this. Do it anyway.
5 min
PHASE 02
WORK BLOCK
THE ONE THREAD — MOVE IT FORWARD
peak cognitive hours
Athanor: one unit of content
One entry, one essay section, one structured page — completed and committed. Not started. Completed. The pattern to interrupt: building infrastructure before content exists. The principle: populate before systematizing. This is the one non-negotiable forward-movement act. Ghostwriting or client work satisfies this slot when it applies.
60–90 min
Reading — primary source only
Adler, Jung, von Franz, Hillman, Eckhart, Pagels, Patanjali, Manly P. Hall — or the Trivium sequence (start with Adler's How to Read a Book). No summaries, no secondary commentary unless you've read the source. The precision-skeptic register requires actual engagement with claims at their origin.
20–30 min
Music project — one session unit
The darkwave piece doesn't finish by thinking about it. One defined task: reverb pass, LMMS arrangement segment, mix review. Pick one before sitting down. This is the creative pressure-release valve that keeps the psychological work from becoming purely discursive. Do this at minimum 3x/week.
30–45 min
PHASE 03
MID-DAY INTERRUPT
REGULATORY RESET — MANDATORY
midday or between blocks
Physical reset — leave the chair
Walk. Stretch. Eat real food with awareness of eating it. This is not optional recovery — it is the maintenance of the biological substrate on which every other project depends. The cleansing protocol you're running demands you take the body seriously as infrastructure, not as an afterthought.
20–30 min
EFT or somatic discharge — if charge is present
Not every day. But when activation is high — when psychological work has surfaced material — run an EFT round using the protocol you already have. The reference guide exists. Use it. Unprocessed activation becomes noise in every downstream task.
10–15 min
PHASE 04
EVENING
CLOSE THE LOOP — ORIENT TO TOMORROW
final 60 min before wind-down
Honest accounting — what actually moved?
Three lines maximum. What completed? What didn't? Why — stated plainly, no self-flagellation and no excuse architecture. This is Adlerian teleology applied backward: what did today's behavior actually serve? Patterns become visible here before they calcify.
5 min
Pathology protocol check — parasitology + remediation
Take remediation agents as scheduled. Log any physical shifts — symptom changes, digestive response, energy. The cleansing process is a live experiment. Log it as data. You built the PATHOLOGY sub-hub for this reason — the knowledge should inform the practice.
5 min
Nervous system downregulation — before sleep
Physiological sigh (double inhale, long exhale) or progressive muscle relaxation. Dim the environment. No content that activates threat response in the final hour — this includes deep psychological material, doomscrolling, or complex production work. Sleep quality directly determines whether tomorrow's protocol is executable.

OPERATING AXIOMS
The archive expands from output, not from planning. One completed unit daily beats ten elaborate sessions monthly.
Your expansion-stagnation loop triggers when too many threads are open simultaneously. This curriculum closes threads by forcing daily completion.
The body is not the metaphor. The cleansing, the breathwork, the somatic checks — these are infrastructure work on the system that runs everything else.
Writing is the practice. The depth practitioner practice grows through written expression first — one essay per month minimum before building infrastructure.
Grounding means: knowing where you actually are, not where you wish you were. The morning question and the evening accounting serve this function.
Direction means: one thread per day has a completion event. Not progress — completion. This is teleological hygiene.
