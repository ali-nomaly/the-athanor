The Body as Temple — Microcosmic Doctrine
The Skull as Heavenly Vault The cranial dome mirrors the celestial sphere. The twelve cranial nerves correspond to the twelve zodiacal signs radiating from the brain-sun.
The Spine as World Axis The vertebral column is the Caduceus, the axis mundi. The thirty-three vertebrae correspond to the degrees of Masonic initiation and the levels of Jacob's Ladder.
The Heart as Sanctum The heart is the solar altar within the temple-body, the seat of the Divine Spark (Pneuma). It corresponds to Tiphareth on the Tree of Life — the mediating center of all forces.
Right vs. Left Polarity The right side is solar, active, masculine (Jachin), governed by the Sun and Mars. The left side is lunar, receptive, feminine (Boaz), governed by the Moon and Venus.
Breath as Sacred Fire Respiration is the perpetual temple rite. The lungs are the twin bellows of the alchemical furnace; inhalation receives divine fire, exhalation returns it purified.
The Eyes as Luminaries The right eye is the Sun (active, rational, diurnal). The left eye is the Moon (passive, intuitive, nocturnal). Together they form the "All-Seeing Eye" in the adept.
