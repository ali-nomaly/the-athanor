The Four Stages — Inner & Outer Meaning
NIGREDOThe Blackening
Ego death, shadow encounter, crisis. The raw undifferentiated self. Confusion, despair, confrontation with the repressed. In media: dungeons, winter, imprisonment, madness, loss. The hero at their lowest.
ALBEDOThe Whitening
Purification, reflection, the inner feminine/masculine emerging. The soul washed clean. Meeting the anima or animus. In media: silver light, the wise guide, water imagery, mirrors, white garments, dawning clarity after chaos.
CITRINITASThe Yellowing
Illumination, solar wisdom, the Wise Old Man/Woman archetype appearing. Integration of insight into will. In media: golden light, teachers, mountaintop revelation, the peacock's iridescence, sunrise, new purpose.
RUBEDOThe Reddening
Coniunctio — sacred marriage of opposites. Full embodiment of the Self. Wholeness lived, not just glimpsed. In media: the return home, the king crowned, red fire, lovers united, the phoenix, sacrifice that completes.

Key Dual Symbols — Sol & Luna
☉ Sol (Sun / Gold / Red King) — Consciousness, the ego, rational mind, the masculine pole. The fixed, solar principle seeking embodiment.

☽ Luna (Moon / Silver / White Queen) — The unconscious, the soul, intuition, the feminine pole. Reflective, receptive, dream-bearing.

Their coniunctio — the sacred marriage — produces the Rebis (the divine hermaphrodite), the Philosopher's Stone: the fully integrated Self, holding both poles without collapsing either.
