Field Manual — Daily Maintenance Protocol
Core principle: The body is not a temple to be worshipped — it's a vehicle to be maintained. Ancient systems mapped the same physiology in different languages: agni = metabolic fire, jing = cellular reserve, wei qi = immune boundary, ojas = nervous system vitality. Modern science has now caught up with nearly all of it.
01

DAWN IGNITION

4:30 – 7:00 AM · Brahma Muhurta · Yang Rising
Ayurveda · Oral

Tongue Scraping

7 strokes front-to-back with a copper or stainless scraper before anything touches the mouth. Removes ama (overnight bacterial coating) and activates the oral-digestive reflex.

Reduces pathogenic bacteria load before it gets swallowed. Copper is antimicrobial by contact.
Ayurveda · Oral

Oil Pulling

1 tbsp sesame or coconut oil, swish 10–15 min while you do other things. Spit in trash (not sink). Rinse with warm salt water.

Emulsifies fat-soluble toxins from gum tissue. Reduces Streptococcus mutans. Supports oral microbiome over brushing alone.
Ayurveda · Hydration

Warm Water + Ginger/Lemon

500ml warm (not hot) water with fresh ginger slices or lemon. First input of the day primes bile production and peristalsis before food.

Kickstarts agni. Lemon raises gastric acid pH prep. Ginger is proven prokinetic — moves things along mechanically.
Ayurveda · Nasal

Nasya + Jala Neti

Neti pot with warm saline first (rinse). Then 2 drops warm sesame or nasya oil in each nostril, inhale gently. Apply mornings in dry or polluted air.

Lubricates mucosal barrier — first line of immune defense. Modern ENT research confirms daily saline irrigation reduces sinus infections significantly.
TCM · Qi Circulation

Qigong / Joint Rotations

5–10 min of systematic joint circles: ankles → knees → hips → spine → shoulders → neck → wrists. Eight Brocades (Ba Duan Jin) is the OG sequence.

Moves synovial fluid into cartilage before load-bearing. Activates meridian pathways. Modern: reduces morning cortisol spike, improves HRV.
TCM · Modern · Light

Morning Sunlight

10–20 min outside within 30 min of wake. No sunglasses. Even overcast works. This is non-negotiable for the whole system.

Sets circadian clock via retinal melanopsin cells. Triggers serotonin → melatonin cascade. Lowers evening cortisol. Andrew Huberman's #1 protocol — fully validated.
02

ABHYANGA — THE OIL JOB

Before Shower · 10–15 Min · Non-Negotiable Weekly, Ideal Daily
This is the central maintenance ritual. Warm oil + friction = lymphatic pump activation + nervous system downshift + skin barrier fortification. Ayurveda calls it the single most vata-pacifying practice. Modern: stimulates peripheral nervous system, moves interstitial fluid, reduces inflammatory markers.
Technique

Warm Sesame Oil — Full Body

Warm oil slightly (test on wrist). Long strokes on limbs toward heart, circular on joints. Scalp gets special attention. Let sit 5–10 min before shower. Use a dedicated towel.

Sesame penetrates deepest of all carrier oils. Warming = vasodilation before absorption. Toward-heart strokes assist lymphatic return.
Contrast Shower

Hot → Cold Finish

Shower normally to rinse oil. Finish with 30–60 sec cold blast. Don't fight it — breathe through it. The cold is the adaptation signal.

Cold triggers norepinephrine spike (up to 300%). Brown adipose activation. Vagal tone increase. Immune cytokine upregulation. Not a trend — it's physiology.
Dry Brushing Alt.

Garshana (Dry Silk Gloves)

If no time for full abhyanga — 5 min dry silk or natural bristle brushing before shower. Circular motions toward lymph nodes (armpits, groin, collarbone).

Exfoliates dead skin, mechanically pumps lymph. Think of it as manually running the lymphatic system that has no pump of its own.
03

MIDDAY ENGINE

11:00 AM – 2:00 PM · Peak Agni · Pitta Time
Ayurveda · Nutrition

Largest Meal at Noon

The digestive system runs on a circadian schedule. Gastric acid, bile, and enzyme output peak around solar noon. This is when you fuel hardest.

Chronobiology research confirms: same meal eaten at noon vs. 8pm produces better insulin sensitivity, lower triglycerides, and higher satiety hormones.
Ayurveda · Digestion

CCF Tea with Meals

Equal parts Cumin + Coriander + Fennel seeds, simmered 10 min. Sip warm with or after lunch. Replaces cold water during meals which douses agni.

Cumin: digestive enzyme secretion. Coriander: bile flow. Fennel: antispasmodic on gut smooth muscle. Collectively: reduce bloat, gas, sluggish digestion.
Ayurveda · Modern

100 Steps After Eating

Shatapavali — 100 steps (≈5 min walk) after the main meal. Light, not a workout. Just enough to initiate gastric emptying and metabolic response.

Proven to blunt postprandial glucose spike by up to 30%. Better than a walk before the meal. Simple, practical, underutilized.
TCM · Acupressure

ST36 — Zusanli

4 finger-widths below the kneecap, one finger outside the shin bone. Apply firm thumb pressure for 1–2 min per side. Activates digestive + immune function.

"Leg Three Miles" — the TCM powerhouse point. Research: stimulates vagal nerve, increases natural killer cell activity, reduces inflammatory cytokines.
04

EVENING WIND-DOWN

6:00 – 9:00 PM · Vata Transition · Yin Building
Ayurveda · Timing

Light Dinner, Early

Smaller than lunch, ideally by 7pm. Soups, kitchari, cooked vegetables — easy on the digestive fire which is dimming. No raw salads or heavy protein at night.

Time-restricted eating: finish eating 3–4 hrs before sleep. Body rebuilds tissue and detoxes during sleep, not digesting food. Liver detox peaks 1–3am.
Ayurveda · Nervous System

Brahmi Oil Head Massage

Warm brahmi or sesame oil worked into scalp with fingertips — 5 to 10 min. Slow, rhythmic, deliberate strokes. Stimulates Marma points at crown and temples.

Activates parasympathetic. Scalp has dense nerve endings — slow mechanical pressure triggers GABA-mediated calm. Also repairs hair follicle barrier.
Ayurveda · Feet

Padabhyanga — Foot Oil

Warm ghee or sesame on soles of feet before bed. Massage in with firm thumb strokes. Wear socks to sleep if needed. Ancient, effective, drastically underused.

Sole of foot = highest density of nerve endings. Activates parasympathetic cascade, reduces cortisol. Clinical trials show improved sleep quality in elderly. Works for all.
TCM · Modern · Breathwork

Nadi Shodhana / Box Breathing

Alternate nostril breathing 5–10 min OR box breath (4-4-4-4). Do this before bed or during any stress spike. The exhale is the braking system.

Direct vagal nerve stimulation via breath. Slows heart rate, lowers blood pressure, shifts from sympathetic to parasympathetic within 3–5 cycles. Fastest free tool in the kit.
Ayurveda · Posture

Viparita Karani — Legs Up Wall

10–15 min with legs against the wall, hips close in, arms out. This is a yoga nidra gateway. Do it post-dinner, or right before sleep.

Reverses venous stasis in lower extremities. Drains lymph from legs. Gentle inversion calms the nervous system and drops cortisol. No equipment needed.
TCM · Modern · Recovery

Sleep Before 10 PM

The pitta time (10pm–2am) is the body's self-repair window. Miss it and you borrow against your jing reserve. Screens off at 9pm minimum.

HGH peaks in deep sleep cycles before midnight. Glymphatic system (brain lymph drainage) runs primarily in stages 3–4. Every hour before midnight is worth 2 after.

THE SUPPLEMENT STACK

Core Fuels — Daily Input Optimization

Quality matters. Choose standardized extracts, third-party tested. Ayurvedic herbs work best as food-forms (powders in warm milk/water) when possible — not isolated capsules.

300–600mg · Evening · With Fat
HPA axis regulation, cortisol reduction, testosterone support, thyroid T3/T4 optimization. The most studied adaptogen. Take at night — not morning.
🪨
Shilajit (Purified)
200–400mg · Morning · Resin Form
Fulvic acid mineral transport. Mitochondrial CoQ10 amplification. Testosterone and DHEA support. Jing tonic — the Ayurvedic equivalent of trace mineral supplementation for cellular energy.
🫐
Triphala
500mg–1g · Before Bed · Powder in Warm Water
Three fruits (amalaki, bibhitaki, haritaki). Bowel regulator, antioxidant powerhouse, gut microbiome prebiotic, eye health. Rasayana — cellular rejuvenation formula.

Brahmi / Bacopa Monnieri
300mg (55% bacosides) · Morning · With Food
Neuroplasticity, memory consolidation, anxiety reduction. Takes 6–8 weeks to peak. TCM equivalent: He Shou Wu for cognitive longevity. Best long-game brain investment.
🍊
Amla / Amalaki
500mg–1g powder daily · Or fresh fruit
Highest natural Vitamin C (heat-stable tannin-bound form). Collagen synthesis, liver protection, immune modulation, lipid lowering. The Ayurvedic longevity fruit — skip synthetic ascorbic acid.
🌙
Magnesium Glycinate
300–400mg · Night · With Dinner
Most depleted mineral in modern humans. Sleep quality, muscle relaxation, nervous system calm, insulin sensitivity, 300+ enzymatic reactions. The single highest-leverage mineral supplement.
☀️
Vitamin D3 + K2
3000–5000 IU D3 · 100–200mcg K2-MK7 · Morning
D3 without K2 is incomplete. K2 directs calcium into bone (not arteries). D3 is immune master regulator, mood, testosterone cofactor. Test your levels — optimize to 50–80 ng/mL.
🐟
Omega-3 (EPA/DHA)
2–3g combined EPA+DHA · With Largest Meal
Cell membrane fluidity, brain structure, anti-inflammatory prostaglandin cascade, cardiovascular, joint lubrication. Use triglyceride-form fish oil or algae (vegan). Refrigerate.
🌾
Turmeric + Black Pepper + Fat
500–1g curcumin · Always with piperine + fat
Curcumin alone is poorly absorbed. Piperine (black pepper) increases bioavailability 2000%. Fat-soluble. Anti-inflammatory, liver protection, joint health. Golden milk formula hits all three.
🔴
Chyawanprash
1–2 tsp · Morning · With Warm Milk or Alone
Ayurvedic polyherbal jam with 40+ botanicals. Amla base + ashwagandha, shatavari, pippali, ghee, honey. The OG comprehensive tonic. Replace your multivitamin with this.
🦠
Probiotic / Fermented Foods
Daily · Vary strains · Food first
Microbiome is the second brain. Lactobacillus and Bifidobacterium strains regulate mood via gut-brain axis, train immune response, produce neurotransmitters. Food: kefir, lassi, miso, kimchi.
🌺
Saffron (Crocus sativus)
30mg daily · Morning
Underrated. Clinical trials show comparable efficacy to SSRIs for mild depression. Protects retina from blue light. Anti-inflammatory. TCM: moves qi, brightens spirit. Tiny dose — huge effect.

Anti-inflammatory trinity. Daily dose in cooking, teas, or golden milk. Non-negotiable base layer.

Bone Broth / Mushroom Broth
Collagen, glycine, proline for gut lining + joint repair. Shiitake/reishi broth for immune beta-glucans. TCM: builds blood and marrow.

Blood, Jing & Vitality Foods

Ayurvedic reproductive tonic. Iron, quick glycogen, tryptophan for serotonin. TCM: nourishes yin, builds blood.
Beets
Nitric oxide precursor via nitrates → endothelial vasodilation. Liver phase 2 detox support. Performance + cardiovascular.
Pomegranate
Punicalagins: the most potent antioxidant in food. Urolithin-A (gut microbiome convert) for mitochondrial renewal. TCM: astringes qi.
Seaweed (Nori, Kelp, Wakame)
Iodine for thyroid, ocean mineral spectrum, fucoidan for immune. TCM: softens hardness, resolves phlegm accumulation.

TOPICALS — EXTERNAL APPLICATIONS

The Skin is the Largest Organ · Everything Penetrates

Sesame Oil — Base Carrier

Full Body · Abhyanga · Daily

Warmest, deepest-penetrating carrier. Sesamin + sesamolin are antioxidants unique to sesame. Vata-pacifying — grounds and lubricates the nervous system through the skin.

Castor Oil — The Detox Pack

Liver · Joints · Scalp · 3x/week

Applied over liver (right side, below ribs) with warm cloth pack — draws out stagnation, supports lymphatic drainage. On joints for inflammation. On scalp for hair follicle regeneration. Ricinoleic acid is deeply anti-inflammatory.

Brahmi Oil — Neural Repair

Scalp + Temples · Evening

Bacopa infused in sesame or coconut. Works through scalp marma points. Cools pitta in the head, calms excess mental activity. Legitimate CNS calming effect via topical absorption.

Neem Oil — Antimicrobial

Skin Conditions · Scalp · Spot Treatment

Azadirachtin compound is antifungal, antibacterial, anti-parasitic. For scalp issues, acne, skin infections. Always dilute 1:10 in carrier. Strong smell — use at night.

Kumkumadi Oil — Face

Face · Nightly · 3–5 drops

Saffron-infused Ayurvedic facial oil with 16+ botanicals. Brightens, repairs barrier, reduces hyperpigmentation. The Ayurvedic retinol analog — takes weeks but works systemically. Apply after face wash at night.

Mahanarayan Oil — Deep Muscle

Joints · Spine · Post-Workout · As Needed

Sesame base with 50+ herbs including ashwagandha, shatavari, bala. Penetrates deeply into muscle and joint tissue. The Ayurvedic equivalent of a deep tissue treatment. Warm before applying.

Magnesium Oil (Transdermal)

Legs · Feet · Post-Workout

Magnesium chloride in water. Applied to skin bypasses gut absorption issues. Spray on sore muscles, calves before sleep, or feet. Muscle cramp relief within 20 min. Pair with oral glycinate for full coverage.

Copper Water (Tamra Jal)

Drink · Store in Copper Vessel Overnight

Store filtered water in copper vessel 8 hrs minimum. Small amount of copper ions transfer. Ayurvedic antimicrobial, antioxidant. Modern: oligodynamic effect of copper on pathogens confirmed by WHO studies on water purification.
